Data Deletion Instructions for SideMatch

```

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of data related to the mobile game SideMatch, developed by AVV Singeo Studio.

Account and Game Data

SideMatch does not create its own user accounts and does not store personal account data on servers operated by AVV Singeo Studio.

The game may use Google Play Games Services for leaderboards and game-related features. If you use Google Play Games Services, your Google Play Games profile, leaderboard scores, achievements, and related activity may be processed by Google.

The game may also use Google AdMob to show ads. Advertising-related data may be processed by Google according to Google's own privacy policy.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to request deletion of data associated with your use of SideMatch, please contact us by email.

Email:
chefalex4590@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • The name of the app: SideMatch
  • A clear request to delete your data
  • Your Google Play Games player name or ID, if your request relates to leaderboard data
  • Any extra details that can help us identify the relevant data

What Data Can Be Deleted

AVV Singeo Studio can review and respond to data deletion requests related to SideMatch. If data is stored only locally on your device, you can delete it by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

For data processed by Google services, such as Google Play Games Services or Google AdMob, users may also need to manage or delete data directly through their Google account settings.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

We will respond to valid deletion requests within a reasonable time. Some data may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or technical reasons.
```
