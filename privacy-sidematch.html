AVV Singeo Studio built SideMatch as a free mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how the game handles user data.

Information We Collect

SideMatch does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, address, or payment information.

The game may use third-party services that can collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, app activity, crash data, diagnostics, and advertising-related data.

Third-Party Services

SideMatch uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and use data to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

SideMatch may also use Google Play Games Services for leaderboards and game-related features. Google Play Games Services may process information related to your Google Play Games account, game progress, scores, achievements, and leaderboard activity.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Google handles data:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertising

SideMatch may show ads, including rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may provide in-game benefits when watched.

Children's Privacy

SideMatch is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the game, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Sharing

SideMatch does not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play Games Services according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Retention

SideMatch may store game-related data locally on the user's device, such as best score, settings, and game progress. Third-party services may retain data according to their own policies.

Security

We care about protecting user data, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
